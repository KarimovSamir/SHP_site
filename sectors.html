Our Sectors

Our team has diverse experience in designing and implementation of construction projects of the following types of real estate properties, understanding the expectations and key success criteria, adopting the regulatory requirements and sustainability considerations to the projects

Shopping Malls

Our team was engaged to ten different Shopping Mall projects on various stages of implementation across the Republic of Azerbaijan, as well as the State of Qatar. This experience in designing, construction and operations at various shopping malls have allowed to gain the full proficiency in know how to built a sustainable environment in retail business, basing on the internationally recognized best practices and client orientation approach

Luxury Hotels

SHP have successfully achieved the given task of adaptation of the old building to the high standard requirements of five-star luxury hotel brand- within the limited budget and resources allocation. The compliance of the finished repairing and adaptation works of the 37 floor hotel and residence building to the defined standards of the luxury hotel brand was inspected by two different international companies from UK and Italy. Finally the luxury five-star hotel and premium-class residence was opened for thousands visitors and residents.

Besides of that, SHP was engaged to other three premium hotel projects and completed renovation project works, according to the Client needs

Restaurants and F&B

Completion of over than 50 various food & beverage projects allowed us to fully understand the specifics of technology processes of the cooking operations, as well as the customer-oriented interiors, creating specific environment in different units- from coffee shops and food court units to the signature restaurants
